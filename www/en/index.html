Construction of agricultural objects is a complex of works on the construction of cold and warm buildings for agricultural purposes - including modular structures, which are characterized by a short construction period and require minimal construction and maintenance costs.

These are plants for the processing of agricultural crops (composite feed, oil, cereals, flour, seed), plants for deep processing of grain (alcohol, bioethanol, biodiesel, biogas, gluten, citric acid, starches, etc.), farms, hangars for planting, processing, irrigation and harvesting equipment, warehouses and granaries, elevators, poultry houses, cowsheds, and other agricultural buildings - modular and capital.

Due to the fact that in the course of construction we use exclusively ecological materials that meet all sanitary standards, as well as apply modern construction technologies, agricultural objects from Axis Core are distinguished by an affordable price, the highest quality and ease of use, while the speed of construction also increases significantly , which is especially important in the case of seasonal work.

Axis Core specialists will make all the necessary measurements on site and build the right object with the right functionality in the shortest possible time!

The company Axis Core offers you the services of a general designer and general contractor in the field of construction of industrial facilities of any complexity. Today, Axis Core is one of the leaders in the construction of industrial facilities throughout Ukraine.
